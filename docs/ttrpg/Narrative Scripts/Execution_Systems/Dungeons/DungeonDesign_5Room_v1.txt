Room 1: Entrance & Guard

Justification: This room serves as the entry point to the adventure or a specific area, and the presence of a guard adds a sense of security or challenge.
Preservation & Hero Role: Traps, hidden entrances, special keys, guardians (golems, robots), foreign guardians, hazards (radiation, clearance, fire) can be incorporated to preserve the room and highlight the heroic abilities of the players. These elements make it necessary for the players to overcome obstacles to progress.
Mood/Theme: The mood/theme of this room could be one of mystery, danger, or security depending on the specific elements incorporated.
Room 2: Puzzle/Role-Playing Challenge

Justification: This room presents a non-combat trial where players must rely on problem-solving and role-playing skills rather than direct confrontation.
Preservation & Hero Role: The preservation of this room lies in the variety of gameplay it offers and the spotlight it puts on the players. Chessboard puzzles, befriending AI, buzzer panels, suspicious concierge, snake-infested floors, and persuading bouncers can be included as challenges.
Mood/Theme: The mood/theme of this room could be one of intellectual challenge, intrigue, or diplomacy.
Room 3: Trick/Setback

Justification: This room introduces a twist or setback, increasing tension and testing the players' resilience.
Preservation & Hero Role: Hostile prisoners, collapsed structures, one-way exits, broken artifacts, NPC betrayal, weakening of PCs can serve as the setbacks or tricks to preserve the room. The challenges should cater to different player types.
Mood/Theme: The mood/theme of this room could be one of suspense, deception, or adversity.
Room 4: Climax/Battle

Justification: This room represents the final encounter or a significant battle that tests the players' skills and provides a climactic moment.
Preservation & Hero Role: The preservation of this room lies in providing memorable tactical and terrain elements. Unexpected powers, prep time, unusual settlements, trapped lairs, reveal rewards, exploitable weaknesses, and varied PC skills can be incorporated. The roleplay interactions with the main antagonist and their minions can enhance the heroic role of the players.
Mood/Theme: The mood/theme of this room could be one of high stakes, intensity, or epic confrontation.
Room 5: Reward/Revelation/Plot Twist

Justification: This room serves as the culmination of the adventure, offering rewards, revelations, or unexpected plot twists.
Preservation & Hero Role: Complications, twists, or changes in location can be introduced to keep the players engaged. The room can contain quest objects, valuable loot, important information, or reveal unexpected connections. Guardians in the treasure, resurrected traps, bonus treasures, rival interference, complicated rewards, helping villains, personal connections, dark truths, changed perceptions, or prophecy revelations can be included.
Mood/Theme: The mood/theme of this room could be one of culmination, revelation, or unexpected consequences.
