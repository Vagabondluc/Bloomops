# Megadungeon Planner

## Objective
Design a large-scale, campaign-spanning dungeon with multiple interconnected levels and evolving challenges.

## Instructions
1. Develop Overarching Concept:
   - Create a central theme or story for the megadungeon
   - Determine dungeon's origin and purpose

2. Plan Dungeon Structure:
   - Outline 10-20 main levels
   - Sketch connections between levels

3. Design Level Themes:
   - Assign unique theme to each level
   - Ensure themes progress logically in difficulty

4. Create Faction Framework:
   - Develop 3-5 major factions
   - Determine territory control and inter-faction dynamics

5. Establish Ecosystem:
   - Design dungeon's internal economy and resource flow
   - Plan for creature habitats and food chains

6. Develop Storylines:
   - Create multiple quest arcs spanning several levels
   - Design secrets and mysteries to uncover

7. Plan Difficulty Progression:
   - Gradually increase challenges deeper into dungeon
   - Include occasional "breather" areas for recovery

8. Design Unique Features:
   - Create signature elements for the megadungeon
   - Include legendary locations or artifacts

9. Implement Restoration Mechanics:
   - Design systems for dungeon restocking
   - Plan how cleared areas might change over time

10. Create Campaign Integration:
    - Design hooks to lead players into megadungeon
    - Plan for occasional outside adventures
