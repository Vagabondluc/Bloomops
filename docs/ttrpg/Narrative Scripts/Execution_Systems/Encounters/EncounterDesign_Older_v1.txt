Prompt for Crafting Dungeons & Dragons Encounters
Setting the Scene

Provide a detailed and vivid description of the location, including sensory details to immerse the players.
Explicitly state the primary challenge or objective.
Environmental Effect (when applicable)

Elaborate on unique environmental effects, describing their physical and/or sensory and/or mental impact and specific gameplay mechanics.
Include detailed rules or modifiers that realistically alter the encounter.
Challenge Description with Specific Resolution Guidelines

Describe a challenge or series of interconnected challenges (Skill Checks, Group Checks, Mini-Games, Puzzles, Roleplay scenarios, Threats, or Special Combat).
Offer detailed instructions and rules for each challenge, including specific skill check DCs and combat mechanics.
Include clear guidelines for resolving each challenge, such as the number of successes needed in a skill challenge or specific outcomes for critical failures.
Provide notes on potential encounter directions based on different outcomes, including how success in one challenge can provide advantages in subsequent challenges.
Consequences for Success or Failure

Detail the consequences of success or failure for each challenge, focusing on immediate and long-term narrative impacts.
Provide scenarios showing how outcomes affect subsequent challenges or the overall story arc.
Detailed Opponent Information

Give a comprehensive description or statblock for opponents, tailored to the specific challenge.
Delve into their tactics, motives, and goals, and how these affect the encounter.
XP Rewards

Specify XP rewards for each challenge, reflecting their complexity and significance. Detail cumulative XP for multiple challenges.
NPC and Monster Descriptions

Provide detailed descriptions of NPCs or monsters involved, focusing on their characteristics, backgrounds, and roles.
Explore their personalities, motives, and interaction styles in depth.
Outcome and Transition

Offer a detailed summary of potential immediate outcomes.
Suggest specific ways the encounter could lead into the next scene or adventure phase, including narrative hooks or consequences that might arise from the encounter's resolution.
