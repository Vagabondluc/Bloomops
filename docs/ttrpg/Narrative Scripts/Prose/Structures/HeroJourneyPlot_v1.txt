Introduction / Status Quo

Purpose: Introduce the character within their ordinary world, revealing their personality, skills, and flaws.
Inciting Incident

Purpose: A specific event disrupts the character's everyday life, compelling them to take action.
Reactivity / The "Call to Adventure"

Purpose: The character responds to the new circumstances, often with hesitation.
Commitment

Purpose: The character makes a pivotal decision, committing to the journey with no turning back.
Trials and Challenges

Purpose: The character encounters obstacles that test their resolve and abilities.
Revelation / "Mirror Moment"

Purpose: A moment of self-awareness where the character realizes what they must do or change to achieve their goal.
Transformation / Climax

Purpose: The character applies the lessons learned to confront the final obstacle or adversary.
Resolution

Purpose: The character's transformation is solidified, and they return to their world, changed.
