Video Summary: Crafting & Publishing RPG Adventures

Seth Skorkowsky offers invaluable insights into creating and publishing RPG adventure modules, treating them as a "recipe" for Game Masters. Discover key strategies for clarity, ease of use, and player engagement.

Key Takeaways:

  • Wording & Terminology: Precise language, active voice, conciseness.
  • In-Game Clarity: Bullet points, side boxes, bold mechanics, page number references.
  • GM's Opening Info: Summaries, character recommendations, required books, NPCs, ideal endings.
  • Flexible Hooks: Multiple entry points and clue delivery methods.
  • Avoid Railroading: Design for player agency and consequences.
  • Playtesting & Editing: Rigorous testing and professional editing are crucial.
  • IP & Locations: Mindful use of existing material and real-world settings.
  • Presentation: Clear maps (GM/Player versions), concise read-alouds, player handouts.
