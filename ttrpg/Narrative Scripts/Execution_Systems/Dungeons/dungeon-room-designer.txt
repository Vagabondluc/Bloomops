# Dungeon Room Designer

## Objective
Create diverse and engaging rooms for your dungeon adventure.

## Instructions
1. Determine Room Type:
   - Scenic: Atmosphere and flavor
   - Challenge: Traps, puzzles, or skill tests
   - Combat: Encounter areas
   - Twist: Surprising elements or revelations
   - Reward: Treasure or important discoveries

2. Define Room Dimensions:
   - Decide on shape (square, circular, irregular)
   - Set dimensions (consider map scale)

3. Describe Base Features:
   - Detail walls, floor, and ceiling
   - Note any architectural elements

4. Add Key Elements:
   - Include at least three interactive elements
   - Ensure elements fit room type and purpose

5. Incorporate Sensory Details:
   - Address at least three senses
   - Include two irrelevant-but-cool details

6. Design Challenges or Encounters:
   - For challenge rooms, create puzzle or skill test
   - For combat rooms, plan enemy placement and tactics

7. Place Treasure or Rewards:
   - Decide on appropriate treasures or information
   - Determine how rewards are hidden or protected

8. Consider Player Interaction:
   - Anticipate possible player actions
   - Prepare for common skill checks or spells

9. Write Room Description:
   - Craft engaging introductory text
   - Detail individual elements thoroughly

10. Review and Refine:
    - Ensure room fits overall dungeon theme
    - Check for balance and player engagement
