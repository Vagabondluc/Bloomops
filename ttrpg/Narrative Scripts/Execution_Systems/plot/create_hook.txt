# Scenario Hook Generator Guidelines

## Types of Scenario Hooks
1. Patronage: An NPC requests a specific action.
2. Offer: Information is presented, leaving the choice to act up to the PCs.
3. Confrontation: The scenario directly encounters the PCs.
4. Dilemma Hook: Multiple hooks in conflict, forcing meaningful choices.
5. Twisted Hook: Misleads PCs about the situation or required action.
6. Meaningful Hook: Ties into PC backgrounds, interests, or ongoing plot threads.
7. Long-Term Hook: Built up over time through multiple interactions or pieces of information.
8. Bait Hook: Designed to position PCs for the real scenario.
9. Treasure Map: Provides guidance for exploration-based scenarios.
